Clearwater Kayak Outfitters Video of the Month

Every year, Clearwater Kayak Outfitters sponsors several extreme kayaking adventure trips for experienced kayakers. Some of our past trips have taken us to the Gauley River in West Virginia, Rogue River in Oregon, Salt River in Arizona, Snake River in Idaho, and Kern River in California. We're thinking about adding an international trip if there's enough interest.

This month's feature video is a compilation from several recent trips, created by Scott Dunsmuir.

Join us on our next adventure and you can be featured in one of our videos of the month!
